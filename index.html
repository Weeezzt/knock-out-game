Knock out game

How to play

  • Pick a number between 6 and 9.
  • When pressing the button two separate dice will roll.
  • If the combined numbers of the dice equal the players chosen number the player gets knockout and looses. If not he keeps playing and the number is added to total points.
  • The goal is to get the highest total points without getting knockout!
  • Most importantly have fun.
